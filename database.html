Hospital Database

I Designed an SQL database with the following requirements:

Patient and Services Management for a Large Hospital
Hospitals house several specializations in medicine and provide routine medical treatment and services (surgery, medical examinations, etc.) to the public. Hospitals also typically have "In- patient" and "Out-patient" departments and at least one pharmacy. Bills are raised on patients for each service provided (by various doctors and labs) and a consolidated statement as well as a consolidated history produced for each patient visit. This system must provide support for the above operations

    The steps taken to develop a working Hospital Database started out with planning first. I created a schema which then became a fully realized EER-Diagram. After many iterations on this schema and EER-diagram the last step was to create the tables and connect them with their relationships using primary and foreign keys. Making sense of ternary realtionships to develop a 3NF compliant database was key.
