SmartFood

For many who have pets,

Whenever you leave for vacation or for many hours, you have to leave extra food for your pets. There’s a high chance that your pet may have made a mess or ran out of food OR What if an emergency arrives and you are unable to provide sufficient food for your pet(s)? There the idea for the Smart Food dispenser was born!

The Smart Food Dispenser is a smart food dispenser made just for pets! You no longer have to leave extra food, you can now feed your pets at the tip of your hands, whenever you want, at whatever time! Using Alexa’s Voice Technology, you are able to feed your pet by saying “Alexa, feed the cat…”

    • Design Process

    In designing the prototype, I had to figure out a way to put together the bowl and feeding container into one. I also had to have the right measurements in order to successfully create the right spinning plate that would transfer the food into the bowl. Everything was modeled in CREO CAD which would later be 3-D Printed, except for the servo powering the mechanism to dispense food.
